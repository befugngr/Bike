About Us

Mike's Bikes of Folsom is located in the Historic District and surrounded by some of the best cycling in the area. Visit our shop for the widest selection of bikes from renowned brands like Cannondale, Santa Cruz, Cervelo, Giant, Gazelle, and Pivot Cycles. Whatever terrain suits you best, we have the road bike, mountain bike, kids bike, or electric bike for you. Our expert bike techs are ready to provide top-notch tune-ups, and don't forget to check our event calendar for exciting group rides in the area.
